Campfire S'mores Smash

Description

Calling all s'mores lovers! The Campfire S'mores Smash is a deconstructed take on the classic treat, perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth without the campfire hassle. Imagine a bed of crushed graham crackers, like a delightful edible dirt, topped with gooey marshmallow fluff and chunks of your favorite chocolate bar. It's a textural explosion in every bite, combining the sweet crunch of graham cracker with the rich chocolate and pillowy marshmallow â€“ all the flavors of a s'more in a fun and mess-free way!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 (10 ounce) package mini marshmallows
  • 2 individual packages graham crackers, crumbled
  • 2 (1.5 ounce) bars chocolate candy bars, broken into pieces

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a cast iron pan over the slow, red embers of your campfire. Pour in marshmallows, and stir until completely melted. Remove from the fire, and stir in the graham crackers and chocolate. Press into the pan with the back of a spoon. Allow to cool completely, then cut into wedges.
