Sugared Campfire Donuts

Description

Sugared campfire donuts are bite-sized bundles of fun, perfect for sharing around a crackling fire. Imagine warm, golden donuts fried over a campfire, then dunked in a blanket of sparkling sugar, with a hint of cinnamon for warmth. Simple and delicious, they're a taste of childhood camping trips condensed into a sweet, unforgettable treat.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated biscuit dough
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Steps

  1. Toss together the sugar and cinnamon in a bowl, set aside. Cut each biscuit into thirds, and roll into balls. Skewer on a toasting fork, and cook over hot coals, turning constantly, until golden brown. Dip into melted butter, then roll in cinnamon sugar.
